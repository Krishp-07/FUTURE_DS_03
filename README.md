## Overview

This project presents a **Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** using the **Bank Marketing Dataset**. The dashboard analyzes prospect conversions, customer segments, contact methods, and age-group performance to uncover key conversion trends and support data-driven marketing decisions.

The dashboard helps businesses evaluate campaign effectiveness, identify high-converting customer segments, and optimize marketing strategies to improve customer acquisition and conversion performance.

---

## Business Problem

Marketing teams need visibility into:

* Conversion performance across campaigns
* Customer segments with the highest conversion potential
* Effectiveness of different contact methods
* Funnel performance and conversion trends
* Opportunities to improve customer acquisition strategies

This dashboard provides actionable insights to support informed marketing decisions.

---

## Key Insights

* Overall conversion rate reached **47.38%**.
* Cellular contact generated **88% of all conversions**.
* Customers aged **25–34** converted the most.
* Management and technician customer segments delivered strong conversion performance.
* Nearly half of all prospects were successfully converted.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Prioritize Cellular Contact Campaigns

Cellular communication demonstrated the highest conversion performance and should receive increased focus.

### Target High-Converting Age Groups

Customers aged 25–34 represent the most responsive audience segment and should be prioritized.

### Focus on High-Performing Customer Segments

Management and technician customer groups showed strong conversion potential and should be targeted with personalized campaigns.

### Optimize Lower-Performing Channels

Review campaign strategies for contact methods with lower conversion rates to improve overall effectiveness.

### Improve Funnel Efficiency

Analyze customer drop-off points and refine campaign messaging to increase conversion rates.
